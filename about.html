About Us

Driving Global Trade Forward

Hong Kong-based premium automobile export company with a decade of trusted service.

Our Story

From Hong Kong to the World

Wah-Pak Global Trading Limited（華博環球貿易有限公司）was established in Hong Kong — one of the world's most dynamic trading hubs. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to bridging the gap between premium automobile markets and global buyers.

What began as a small trading operation has grown into a full-service automobile export enterprise. Our strategic location in Hong Kong provides us with unique advantages: world-class banking infrastructure, efficient logistics networks, and a business-friendly regulatory environment that enables seamless international trade.

Over the years, we have built lasting relationships with dealerships, auction houses, and logistics partners across Asia, enabling us to source the best vehicles at the most competitive prices. Our clients range from independent dealerships to large-scale importers across Africa, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide a reliable, transparent, and efficient automobile export service that empowers businesses worldwide to access quality vehicles at fair prices — with complete peace of mind.
