FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What markets do you serve?

We primarily serve clients across Africa, the Middle East, South Asia, and Southeast Asia. Our Hong Kong base allows efficient shipping to most global destinations.

What is the minimum order quantity?

There is no strict minimum — we handle everything from single-unit purchases to bulk shipments of 50+ vehicles. Contact us with your needs for a tailored quote.

How do you ensure vehicle quality?

Every vehicle undergoes a comprehensive multi-point inspection by certified technicians. We provide detailed condition reports and high-resolution photos before shipment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Letter of Credit (L/C), Telegraphic Transfer (T/T), and can discuss other arrangements for established clients. Our Hong Kong banking infrastructure ensures secure transactions.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by destination. Typical transit ranges from 2-6 weeks depending on the port and shipping method (RO-RO vs container). We provide estimated timelines with every quote.

Do you handle import documentation?

We provide all export documentation from Hong Kong. For import-side requirements, we work closely with your local clearing agent to ensure all documents meet destination country regulations.
